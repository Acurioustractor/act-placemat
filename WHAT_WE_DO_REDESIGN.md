# Redesigning "WHAT We Do" - More Nuance & Impact

## Current Problem
The current "WHAT" section is too simple:
- Just lists themes with counts
- Doesn't show the **intersectionality** of the work
- Doesn't communicate **why** this matters
- Misses the **complexity** and **sophistication** of ACT's approach

## Better Approach: "Weaving Narratives of Change"

Instead of just themes, show:
1. **What the work actually IS** (with concrete examples)
2. **How themes intersect** (most projects span multiple themes)
3. **The deeper purpose** (not just "storytelling" but "community-controlled narratives")
4. **Real outcomes** (what changes because of this work)
