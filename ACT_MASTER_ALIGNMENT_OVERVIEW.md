## 1. Core Identity & Philosophy
- **Movement, not organisation** ‚Äî ACT embodies a philosophy that proves two people can catalyse global change by becoming unnecessary (`Docs/Strategy/ACT_MASTER_PHILOSOPHY_2025.md:8-33`).
- **Beautiful Obsolescence** is the guiding system: projects (6-24m), organisations (3-7y), and the philosophy itself (7-15y) are designed to hand back power (`Docs/Strategy/ACT_MASTER_PHILOSOPHY_2025.md:20-57`).
- **Values locks** keep us honest: Radical Humility, Creative Disruption, Truth-Telling, Indigenous Wisdom Primacy, Anti-Extraction, Joyful Seriousness (`Docs/Strategy/ACT_MASTER_PHILOSOPHY_2025.md:79-121`).
- **Rocket Booster model** defines the cadence of support ‚Üí independence (`Docs/Strategy/ACT_MASTER_PHILOSOPHY_2025.md:58-77`).
- **True philosophy alignment**: we build systems that make extraction impossible and celebrate adventure, art, curiosity, and giving (`Docs/Strategy/ACT_TRUE_PHILOSOPHY_ALIGNMENT.md:1-82`).

### First-Principles Checklist
1. **Does this increase community sovereignty?** (`Docs/Strategy/ACT_DATA_PHILOSOPHY_INTEGRATION.md:5-44`)
2. **Are we handing over power on an explicit timeline?** (`Docs/Strategy/ACT_MASTER_PHILOSOPHY_2025.md:275-308`)
3. **Are stories owned and shared by the communities involved?** (`Docs/Strategy/ACT_TRUE_PHILOSOPHY_ALIGNMENT.md:11-34`)
4. **Are we removing extractive economics and replacing them with community-owned value?** (`Docs/Strategy/ACT_MASTER_PHILOSOPHY_2025.md:196-233`)
5. **Will this help ACT disappear gracefully while communities thrive?** (`Docs/Strategy/ACT_MASTER_PHILOSOPHY_2025.md:22-57`)

- **Witta Harvest HQ** active project driving regenerative community space with Decentralised Power at its core (`.taskmaster/docs/ACTIVE_STRATEGY/complete-ecosystem/projects.json:2543-2574`).

- **Regional partner web** includes Sunshine Coast councils, universities, and community champions (e.g. `Docs/LinkedIn/Bens_data/Connections.csv:15`, `Docs/LinkedIn/Bens_data/Connections.csv:3597`).

### First-Principles Priorities for Place Work
1. **Country-Led Stewardship** ‚Äî Embed Indigenous governance and cultural protocols (aligns with Locked Value #1) before any infrastructure decisions (`Docs/Strategy/ACT_MASTER_PHILOSOPHY_2025.md:275-308`).
2. **Community Production & Learning** ‚Äî Use Goods., Witta Harvest HQ, Seed House Witta as a living lab for regenerative manufacturing, food systems, and story documentation.
3. **Hospitality & Respite** ‚Äî Position the farm as neutral ground for youth justice partners, storytellers, and Elders to rest, plan, and cross-pollinate ideas.
4. **Data & Story Kiosk** ‚Äî Co-design a local transparency node (case study wall, media library, community briefing screens) that mirrors the Knowledge Wiki for visitors.
5. **Regional Cadence** ‚Äî Map relationships, cadence alerts, and shared opportunity pipeline for Sunshine Coast and Hinterland partners so follow-up is community driven.

### Immediate Experiments
- **Witta Residency Program**: quarterly invitations for partners to co-design or test interventions on-country (e.g. justice reinvestment prototyping weekends).
- **Neighbourhood Knowledge Walks**: document land history, current custodians, ecological indicators to enrich project records and AI context.
- **Local Supplier Ledger**: track cooperative economics (farm inputs, artisans, transport) to model circular spending and inform Goods. expansion.
