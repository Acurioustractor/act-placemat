# Training Materials and Onboarding Workflows

## Overview

This document outlines comprehensive training materials and onboarding workflows for different user types on the ACT Placemat platform. Our approach emphasizes cultural sensitivity, community building, and effective platform utilization while respecting Indigenous data sovereignty principles.

## Onboarding Framework

### Learning Principles

**Culturally Responsive Learning**
- Respect for diverse learning styles and preferences
- Integration of Indigenous knowledge systems
- Community-centered learning approaches
- Emphasis on relationship building and connection

**Progressive Disclosure**
- Start with essential information
- Gradually introduce advanced features
- Build confidence through early successes
- Provide just-in-time learning resources

**Practical Application**
- Hands-on learning with real platform features
- Immediate application of learned concepts
- Peer learning and mentorship opportunities
- Regular practice and skill reinforcement

### Learning Pathways

**Essential Path** (All Users)
- Platform introduction and values
- Basic cultural awareness
- Account setup and privacy settings
- Community guidelines and expectations
- Essential platform features

**Community Member Path**
- Storytelling and content creation
- Community engagement best practices
- Cultural protocol understanding
- Opportunity discovery and applications
- Building meaningful connections

**Community Leader Path**
- Community creation and management
- Moderation and conflict resolution
- Cultural consultation processes
- Event planning and coordination
- Member support and development

**Administrator Path**
- System administration and monitoring
- User support and troubleshooting
- Cultural protocol enforcement
- Crisis management and response
- Platform governance and policy

## New User Onboarding

### Pre-Registration Phase

**Welcome Sequence**
1. **Introduction Video** (3 minutes)
   - Platform purpose and values
   - Community focus and impact
   - Cultural respect and protocols
   - User testimonials and success stories

2. **Expectations Setting**
   - Community-driven platform focus
   - Commitment to cultural respect
   - Time investment for meaningful engagement
   - Benefits of active participation

3. **Cultural Primer** (5 minutes)
   - Acknowledgment of Country explanation
   - Basic Indigenous culture awareness
   - Data sovereignty principles introduction
   - Respectful engagement principles

### Registration and Setup

**Step 1: Account Creation** (10 minutes)
- Choose registration method (email, social login)
- Verify email address and set secure password
- Enable two-factor authentication (recommended)
- Accept terms of service and community guidelines

**Interactive Elements:**
- Password strength indicator
- 2FA setup wizard
- Privacy settings explanation
- Cultural guidelines acknowledgment

**Step 2: Profile Creation** (15 minutes)
- Upload profile photo and basic information
- Select interests and community connections
- Set privacy and communication preferences
- Choose initial communities to join

**Guided Tour:**
- Profile completion checklist
- Privacy settings explanation
- Community discovery tools
- First connection suggestions

**Step 3: Cultural Orientation** (20 minutes)
- Interactive cultural awareness module
- Platform cultural guidelines review
- Appropriate acknowledgment practices
- Sacred knowledge protection understanding

**Activities:**
- Cultural sensitivity quiz
- Acknowledgment of Country practice
- Content sharing guidelines review
- Cultural advisor contact information

## Community Leader Training

### Prerequisites

**Essential Requirements**
- Completed new user onboarding
- Demonstrated platform engagement (30+ days)
- Cultural competency training completion
- Community endorsement or recommendation
- Commitment to ongoing learning and development

**Recommended Experience**
- Community management or leadership experience
- Cultural awareness and sensitivity
- Conflict resolution skills
- Event planning and coordination experience
- Commitment to platform values and mission

### Training Modules

**Module 1: Community Leadership Foundations** (4 hours)

*Learning Objectives:*
- Understand community leadership principles
- Develop inclusive leadership approaches
- Practice conflict resolution techniques
- Learn community building strategies

*Content:*
- Community leadership theory and practice
- Indigenous leadership models and principles
- Inclusive decision-making processes
- Community engagement strategies
- Conflict prevention and resolution

*Activities:*
- Leadership style assessment
- Case study analysis and discussion
- Role-playing conflict scenarios
- Community vision development exercise

**Module 2: Cultural Competency and Protocols** (6 hours)

*Learning Objectives:*
- Master platform cultural protocols
- Develop cultural consultation skills
- Understand data sovereignty implications
- Practice culturally appropriate moderation

*Content:*
- Advanced cultural awareness training
- Indigenous data sovereignty principles
- Cultural content review processes
- Elder consultation protocols
- Community consultation best practices

*Activities:*
- Cultural sensitivity scenarios
- Mock Elder consultation practice
- Content review simulations
- Cultural protocol case studies

**Module 3: Community Management Tools** (3 hours)

*Learning Objectives:*
- Master community management features
- Develop content moderation skills
- Learn member support techniques
- Practice crisis management

*Content:*
- Platform moderation tools and features
- Content guidelines enforcement
- Member onboarding and support
- Crisis communication and management
- Analytics and community health monitoring

*Activities:*
- Hands-on tool exploration
- Moderation scenario practice
- Member support role-playing
- Crisis response planning

**Module 4: Event Planning and Coordination** (4 hours)

*Learning Objectives:*
- Plan culturally appropriate events
- Coordinate online and offline activities
- Manage event logistics and participation
- Evaluate event success and impact

*Content:*
- Event planning principles and practices
- Cultural considerations for events
- Technology tools for event management
- Accessibility and inclusion planning
- Post-event evaluation and improvement

*Activities:*
- Event planning workshop
- Cultural event case studies
- Accessibility audit practice
- Event promotion and marketing

### Practical Application

**Mentorship Phase** (30 days)
- Paired with experienced community leader
- Shadow moderation and management activities
- Gradual responsibility increase
- Regular feedback and guidance sessions

**Supervised Practice** (60 days)
- Independent community management with oversight
- Regular check-ins and support
- Crisis management support availability
- Performance feedback and improvement planning

**Full Independence** (90 days)
- Complete community leadership responsibilities
- Access to ongoing support and resources
- Peer mentorship and collaboration opportunities
- Continuous learning and development programs

## Cultural Competency Training

### Foundation Level (All Users)

**Module 1: Indigenous Australia Overview** (2 hours)
- History and diversity of Indigenous cultures
- Traditional Owner and Custodian recognition
- Contemporary Indigenous Australia
- Acknowledgment of Country practices

**Module 2: Data Sovereignty Principles** (1 hour)
- Indigenous data rights and sovereignty
- CARE principles understanding
- Platform data practices and protections
- Individual and collective data considerations

**Module 3: Respectful Engagement** (1.5 hours)
- Appropriate language and terminology
- Cultural sensitivity in online interactions
- Avoiding cultural appropriation
- Building authentic relationships

### Intermediate Level (Community Leaders)

**Module 4: Traditional Knowledge Systems** (3 hours)
- Understanding Traditional Knowledge frameworks
- Sacred and secular knowledge distinctions
- Gender-specific cultural considerations
- Seasonal and ceremonial protocols

**Module 5: Community Consultation** (2 hours)
- Free, prior, and informed consent principles
- Community decision-making processes
- Elder consultation protocols
- Conflict resolution and mediation

**Module 6: Cultural Content Management** (2 hours)
- Content classification and protection levels
- Review and approval processes
- Attribution and acknowledgment requirements
- Community benefit sharing principles

### Advanced Level (Administrators and Cultural Officers)

**Module 7: Legal and Policy Framework** (4 hours)
- Native Title and Land Rights legislation
- Intellectual property and Traditional Knowledge
- Privacy and data protection laws
- International Indigenous rights frameworks

**Module 8: Cultural Crisis Management** (3 hours)
- Identifying and responding to cultural violations
- Emergency consultation procedures
- Community communication and relationship repair
- Legal and regulatory compliance

**Module 9: Cultural Leadership and Advocacy** (3 hours)
- Supporting Indigenous self-determination
- Advocacy and allyship principles
- Policy development and cultural integration
- Ongoing learning and cultural humility

### Assessment and Certification

**Knowledge Assessment**
- Multiple choice questions on key concepts
- Scenario-based problem solving
- Case study analysis and response
- Cultural sensitivity evaluation

**Practical Assessment**
- Role-playing cultural scenarios
- Mock consultation and decision-making
- Content review and classification practice
- Crisis response simulation

**Certification Levels**
- **Foundation**: Basic cultural awareness
- **Intermediate**: Community leadership competency
- **Advanced**: Cultural administration expertise
- **Expert**: Cultural training and mentorship capability
