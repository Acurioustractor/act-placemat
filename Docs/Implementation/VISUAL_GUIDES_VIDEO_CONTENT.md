## Visual Design Principles

### Cultural Appropriateness

**Indigenous Design Elements**
- Respectful use of cultural motifs with appropriate permissions
- Earth tones and natural color palettes
- Organic shapes and flowing design elements
- Cultural symbols used only with Elder approval

**Inclusive Representation**
- Diverse community representation in imagery
- Multiple age groups, abilities, and backgrounds
- Authentic community photos and testimonials
- Non-stereotypical cultural representation

**Brand Alignment**
- Consistent use of ACT Placemat brand colors and fonts
- Professional yet approachable visual style
- Community-centered imagery and messaging
- Values-driven visual communication

### Accessibility Standards

**Visual Accessibility**
- High contrast color combinations (WCAG AA compliant)
- Large, readable fonts (minimum 16px for body text)
- Clear visual hierarchy and white space
- Alternative text for all images and graphics

**Cognitive Accessibility**
- Simple, clear visual layouts
- Consistent navigation and design patterns
- Logical information flow and organization
- Reduced cognitive load through progressive disclosure

**Motor Accessibility**
- Large touch targets for mobile interfaces
- Clear, distinguishable interactive elements
- Keyboard navigation support
- Voice control compatibility

### Learning-Oriented Design

**Progressive Disclosure**
- Step-by-step visual breakdowns
- Clear progression indicators
- Optional detail expansion
- Summary and review sections

**Multiple Learning Modalities**
- Visual, auditory, and kinesthetic learning support
- Text, image, video, and interactive combinations
- Cultural storytelling integration
- Hands-on practice opportunities

### Video Production Specifications

**Technical Standards**
- 1080p HD resolution minimum
- 16:9 aspect ratio for desktop, 9:16 for mobile
- Clear, professional audio (minimum -12dB)
- Closed captions in English (additional languages as needed)
- Mobile-optimized viewing experience

**Visual Style Guide**
- Consistent branding and color scheme
- Clean, uncluttered backgrounds
- Professional lighting and composition
- Accessible font choices and sizes
- Cultural design elements where appropriate

**Audio Requirements**
- Clear, conversational narration
- Appropriate pacing for comprehension
- Background music (culturally appropriate, non-distracting)
- Sound effects to enhance learning
- Multiple language options where possible

## Cultural Sensitivity in Visual Content

### Indigenous Representation Guidelines

**Respectful Imagery**
- Use images with proper consent and permissions
- Avoid stereotypical or tokenistic representation
- Include diverse Indigenous communities and experiences
- Respect cultural protocols around sacred imagery

**Cultural Elements**
- Use cultural design elements only with approval
- Acknowledge cultural sources appropriately
- Avoid appropriation of sacred symbols
- Seek ongoing cultural advisory input

**Language and Messaging**
- Use respectful and inclusive language
- Avoid outdated or inappropriate terminology
- Include appropriate acknowledgments
- Reflect community voices and priorities

### Community Consultation Process

**Pre-Production Review**
- Submit content concepts to Cultural Advisors
- Allow sufficient time for consultation
- Incorporate feedback and recommendations
- Seek Elder approval for sensitive content

**Production Oversight**
- Include cultural advisors in production process
- Regular check-ins and feedback sessions
- Respect for cultural protocols during filming
- Appropriate acknowledgments and credits

**Post-Production Review**
- Final content review by Cultural Advisors
- Community feedback and testing
- Elder approval for cultural content
- Ongoing monitoring and updates

## Accessibility Standards

### Visual Accessibility

**Color and Contrast**
- WCAG AA compliant color combinations
- High contrast text and background
- Color-blind friendly palettes
- Alternative visual indicators beyond color

**Typography**
- Minimum 16px font size for body text
- Clear, readable font choices
- Appropriate line spacing and margins
- Scalable text for zoom compatibility

**Layout and Design**
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Consistent navigation patterns
- Adequate white space
- Logical content flow

### Video Accessibility

**Captions and Subtitles**
- Accurate closed captions for all videos
- Multiple language options where appropriate
- Synchronized timing and positioning
- Clear, readable caption styling

**Audio Descriptions**
- Detailed audio descriptions for visual content
- Clear narration of on-screen actions
- Description of important visual elements
- Synchronized with video content

**Interactive Elements**
- Keyboard navigation support
- Screen reader compatibility
- Clear focus indicators
- Alternative input methods

### Mobile Accessibility

**Touch Interface**
- Large, easily tappable elements
- Clear spacing between interactive elements
- Gesture alternatives for complex interactions
- Voice control compatibility

**Screen Size Adaptation**
- Responsive design for all screen sizes
- Readable text without horizontal scrolling
- Accessible button and link sizes
- Optimized media playback
