## Contracts: MSA & SOW (Sprint 1)

Owner: Ben  
Due: End of Week 2  
Related Task: 14.15 — Contract templates (MSA/SOW)

Objectives
- Baseline MSA and SOW templates for quick contracting and clear scope
- Redline rules and approval thresholds
- E‑sign flow ready (DocuSign/HelloSign/Notion export → PDF)

MSA sections (outline)
- Parties, Definitions, Services Description (referencing SOW), Term & Termination
- Fees & Invoicing, Taxes, Late Payments
- Confidentiality, Data Protection & Privacy, Security Standards
- IP & License, Open‑source usage, Third‑party services (Xero/Gmail/Notion)
- Warranties, Disclaimers, Limitation of Liability, Indemnity
- Compliance & Ethics, Non‑Solicit (optional), Assignment, Governing Law

SOW sections (outline)
- Scope & Deliverables (e.g., Finance summary, receipts capture, DI setup)
- Timeline & Milestones (Week 0–2), Dependencies (access/owners)
- Acceptance Criteria (per sprint deliverables)
- Fees: Setup + Subscription; Change Requests process

Redline rules (guardrails)
- Liability cap: 1x fees paid in last 12 months (min 3 months)  
- Data/IP: customer owns their data and outputs; we own platform IP  
- Payment terms: Net 14 (Starter/Growth), Net 30 (Partner)  
- Privacy: standard DPA; subprocessors list maintained

E‑sign flow
- Author in Notion → export to PDF → send via e‑sign (DocuSign/HelloSign)  
- Store signed copies in `Contracts/` with metadata in CRM Deal
