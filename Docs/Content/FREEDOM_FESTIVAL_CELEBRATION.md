## üåü Festival Vision

**"Celebrating communities that no longer need us."**

The Freedom Festival is an annual celebration of communities that achieved independence, organizations that worked themselves out of jobs, and the beautiful obsolescence that creates lasting change. It's a festival where the greatest honor is to be forgotten, and the highest achievement is to become unnecessary.

### Core Philosophy
- **Independence celebrated** - Communities that fly on their own power
- **Obsolescence honored** - Organizations that succeeded by becoming unnecessary  
- **Innovation showcased** - Solutions we never imagined communities would create
- **Freedom defined** - Self-determination and community sovereignty
- **Beautiful endings** - How to gracefully become irrelevant
