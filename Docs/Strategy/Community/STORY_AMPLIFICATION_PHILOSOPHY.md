## üìñ The Power of Story Amplification

### What We're Really Building

Not a franchise model, but a **global story network** where:

1. **Australian communities** show the world what's possible
2. **Each community** owns their narrative and journey
3. **Stories inspire** without prescribing methods
4. **Success patterns** spread organically, not systematically
5. **Communities learn** from each other, not from us

### The Story Lifecycle

```
Collect ‚Üí Celebrate ‚Üí Amplify ‚Üí Inspire ‚Üí Disconnect
```

1. **Collect**: Gather authentic stories of community transformation
2. **Celebrate**: Honor the communities doing the work
3. **Amplify**: Share globally through podcasts, books, media
4. **Inspire**: Let others see what's possible
5. **Disconnect**: Step back as communities find their own way
