This platform isn't a product to sell - it's a tool for community empowerment. Our goal isn't to maximize revenue or create dependency. Instead, we want to:

## ü§≤ PARTNERSHIP CONVERSATION GUIDE

### Opening: Relationship First
"Before we look at any technology, tell me - what's the biggest challenge facing your community right now? What would real support look like for you?"

### Sharing the Tool: Community Asset
"We've built this by connecting everyone's knowledge - Ben and Nic's 15,000 LinkedIn connections, 347 stories from 191 storytellers. This belongs to all of us. Let me show you what we've discovered about grant opportunities for organisations like yours..."

### The Sustainability Question
"We want this to be sustainable but not extractive. The platform costs about $2,000/month to run. Some organisations contribute financially, others contribute stories or connections, some help train others. What feels right for you?"

### Building Capacity Together
"More importantly - we want to teach your team to do this themselves. Would you be interested in having someone from your organisation learn alongside us? They could become the grant intelligence expert for your region."

## üìä SHARING THE PLATFORM (Not "Demos")

### Focus on Their Mission First
1. Ask about their current projects and challenges
2. Listen for where data/connections could help
3. Show relevant queries that solve THEIR problems
4. Explore together, don't perform

### Example Conversation Flow:
**You:** "What grants are you working on right now?"
**Them:** "We're struggling with the Knight Foundation application..."
**You:** "Let's explore that together. I'll search for stories that match their criteria..."
*Type query together, discover insights together*

### Make It Collaborative
- "What would you search for?"
- "Does this match your experience?"
- "How could we make this more useful?"
- "Who else should have access to this?"

## üí∞ SUSTAINABLE ECONOMICS (Not "Pricing")

### Sliding Scale Model
Based on organisation capacity, not fixed pricing:

**Large Foundations ($10M+ budget)**
- $2,000-3,000/month contribution
- Helps subsidize smaller orgs
- Gets priority features/support

**Mid-Size Organisations ($1-10M budget)**
- $500-1,000/month contribution
- Or equivalent in-kind contribution
- Access to full platform

**Small Community Groups (<$1M budget)**
- $100-200/month or what they can afford
- Or contribute stories/connections
- Full access, community support

**Individual Grant Writers/Consultants**
- Skill exchange - they help others in return for access
- Build community capacity
- Create local employment

### Alternative Contributions
- Share grant-winning templates
- Contribute stories and impact data
- Train other organisations
- Provide feedback for improvements
- Connect us to other communities
