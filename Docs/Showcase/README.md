## ðŸŒŸ Key Showcase Themes

Based on the comprehensive project analysis, ACT's core showcase themes are:

### **1. Story & Sovereignty**
- **Empathy Ledger** as flagship storytelling platform
- Community ownership and consent-first design
- Traditional story preservation (Wilya Janta Communications)

### **2. Justice & Healing**  
- **BG Fit** - Indigenous youth transformation through culture

- Alternative justice models (Diagrama, First Pathways)

### **3. Health & Regeneration**
- **Goods.** - Community manufacturing revolution
- **Black Cockatoo Valley** - Conservation meets community economics
- **SMART Recovery** - Community-led addiction recovery

### **4. Connection & Innovation**
- **Global Laundry Alliance** - Dignity through clean clothes access
- Interactive installations (Gold.Phone, The Confessional)

### **5. Operations & Infrastructure**
- **Dual-entity structure** - Foundation + Ventures model
- **40% profit guarantee** to communities
- **Design for obsolescence** - tools communities can own
- Mission-locked legal structures

## ðŸŽ¬ Presentation Guidelines

### **ACT's Core Values in Showcases:**
1. **Radical Humility** - Community voices lead, we amplify
2. **Decentralised Power** - Tools that communities can own and fork
3. **Creativity as Disruption** - Art, innovation, and creative solutions
4. **Uncomfortable Truth-Telling** - Honest, unfiltered impact stories

### **Showcase Standards:**
- **Community voices first** - Always lead with participant/partner voices
- **No exaggerated marketing language** - Authentic, honest presentation
- **Data with stories** - Balance metrics with human narratives  
- **Open-source mindset** - Show how others can replicate/fork approaches
- **Impact transparency** - Clear metrics and community benefit sharing
