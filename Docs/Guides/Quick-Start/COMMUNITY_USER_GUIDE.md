## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit the Platform**
   - Go to [actplacemat.org.au](https://actplacemat.org.au)
   - Click "Join Our Community"
   - Choose your preferred sign-up method

2. **Account Verification**
   - Verify your email address
   - Complete identity verification (for enhanced features)
   - Set up your security preferences

3. **Welcome Tour**
   - Take the interactive platform tour
   - Explore community features
   - Set your initial preferences

### First Steps

**Complete Your Profile** (10 minutes)
- Add your basic information
- Upload a profile photo
- Write a brief introduction
- Set your location and interests

**Join Your First Community** (5 minutes)
- Browse available communities
- Read community guidelines
- Request to join or follow communities

**Explore the Platform** (15 minutes)
- Browse recent stories and updates
- Check out community events
- Discover opportunities in your area

## Sharing Your Stories

### Story Types and Formats

**Personal Stories**
- Life experiences and journeys
- Challenges overcome and lessons learned
- Achievements and milestones
- Community connections and relationships

**Community Impact Stories**
- Local initiatives and projects
- Volunteer experiences
- Social change efforts
- Collaborative successes

**Cultural Stories** (with appropriate protocols)
- Cultural heritage and tradition
- Community celebrations and events
- Knowledge sharing (where appropriate)
- Cultural learning experiences

**Multimedia Content**
- Text-based stories and articles
- Photo essays and visual stories
- Audio recordings and podcasts
- Video content and documentaries

### Creating Compelling Stories

**Story Structure**
1. **Opening**: Hook readers with an engaging start
2. **Context**: Provide necessary background information
3. **Journey**: Share the experience or process
4. **Impact**: Describe outcomes and learning
5. **Call to Action**: Invite engagement or participation

**Writing Tips**
- Write in your authentic voice
- Use specific details and examples
- Include emotional elements
- Be honest about challenges and failures
- Respect others' privacy and consent

**Visual Elements**
- Include relevant photos or illustrations
- Ensure you have rights to all images
- Add captions that enhance the story
- Consider accessibility for all users

### Cultural Story Guidelines

**Before Sharing Cultural Content**
1. **Consider the Source**
   - Is this your story to tell?
   - Do you have permission to share?
   - Are there cultural protocols to follow?
   - Who should review this content?

2. **Respect Sacred Knowledge**
   - Some cultural information is not meant to be shared publicly
   - Respect men's and women's business distinctions
   - Consider seasonal or ceremonial restrictions
   - Consult with Elders or cultural advisors when uncertain

3. **Attribution and Acknowledgment**
   - Credit the source of cultural knowledge
   - Acknowledge Traditional Owners and Custodians
   - Respect intellectual property rights
   - Follow community attribution preferences

4. **Community Review Process**
   - Submit cultural content for community review
   - Allow time for Elder consultation if required
   - Accept feedback and suggested modifications
   - Understand that some content may not be approved

### Story Publishing Process

**Draft Creation**
1. Start with a story outline
2. Write your first draft
3. Add multimedia elements
4. Review for accuracy and sensitivity
5. Save as draft for later editing

**Review and Editing**
1. Review content for clarity and flow
2. Check facts and verify information
3. Ensure cultural appropriateness
4. Consider community guidelines
5. Make necessary revisions

**Publication Settings**
1. Choose your audience (public, community, limited)
2. Set content warnings if appropriate
3. Add relevant tags and categories
4. Schedule publication time if desired
5. Enable or disable comments

**Community Review**
- Some content may require community review
- Cultural stories often need Elder approval
- Sensitive topics may need moderation review
- Reviews typically take 1-3 business days

## Cultural Protocols

### Respecting Indigenous Culture

**Acknowledgment of Country**
- Acknowledge Traditional Owners in relevant content
- Use appropriate language and terminology
- Show respect for ongoing connections to Country
- Understand the difference between Welcome and Acknowledgment

**Cultural Sensitivity**
- Research before sharing cultural information
- Respect sacred and secret knowledge
- Understand your role as an ally (if non-Indigenous)
- Learn about local protocols and customs

**Consultation Processes**
- Engage with appropriate cultural advisors
- Allow time for proper consultation
- Respect community decision-making processes
- Accept guidance and feedback gracefully

### Working with Cultural Advisors

**When to Consult**
- Before sharing cultural stories or knowledge
- When unsure about cultural appropriateness
- For community events with cultural elements
- When conflicts arise involving cultural issues

**How to Engage**
- Contact cultural advisors respectfully
- Provide context and specific questions
- Be patient with response times
- Accept guidance and recommendations
- Offer appropriate acknowledgment

### Data Sovereignty

**Understanding Your Rights**
- You control your personal data
- Cultural data has additional protections
- Communities have collective rights
- You can request data deletion or portability

**Responsible Data Practices**
- Read and understand privacy policies
- Make informed choices about data sharing
- Respect others' data sovereignty rights
- Report data misuse or concerns

## Community Guidelines

### Core Principles

**Respect and Dignity**
- Treat all community members with respect
- Value diverse perspectives and experiences
- Avoid discrimination and prejudice
- Support inclusive community culture

**Cultural Sensitivity**
- Respect Indigenous cultures and protocols
- Avoid cultural appropriation
- Seek permission before sharing cultural content
- Acknowledge Traditional Owners appropriately

**Authentic Engagement**
- Be genuine in your interactions
- Share truthful and accurate information
- Respect intellectual property rights
- Give credit where credit is due

**Community Wellbeing**
- Contribute positively to community health
- Support other community members
- Report problems and concerns
- Help maintain safe spaces

### Prohibited Behaviours

**Harassment and Abuse**
- Bullying, intimidation, or threatening behaviour
- Personal attacks or targeted harassment
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Discriminatory language or behaviour

**Inappropriate Content**
- Hate speech or discriminatory content
- Sexually explicit or inappropriate material
- Violence or graphic content
- Spam or commercial promotion without permission

**Cultural Violations**
- Sharing sacred or restricted cultural information
- Cultural appropriation or misrepresentation
- Disrespecting Elders or cultural authorities
- Ignoring cultural protocols and guidelines

**Platform Misuse**
- Creating fake accounts or profiles
- Manipulating voting or engagement systems
- Violating others' privacy or data rights
- Disrupting community discussions or events

### Consequences and Appeals

**Warning System**
1. First violation: Warning and guidance
2. Second violation: Temporary restrictions
3. Third violation: Extended restrictions
4. Serious violations: Immediate suspension or ban

**Appeals Process**
1. Submit appeal with explanation
2. Community review by moderators
3. Cultural advisor consultation if relevant
4. Decision communication within 5 business days
5. Final appeal to platform administrators
