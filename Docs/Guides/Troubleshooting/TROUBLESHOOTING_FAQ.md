## üö® Urgent Issues

### Platform Down or Unavailable

**Problem**: Can't access ACT Placemat website or app
**Quick Solutions**:
1. Check [status.actplacemat.org.au](https://status.actplacemat.org.au) for platform status
2. Try refreshing your browser or restarting the app
3. Check your internet connection
4. Try accessing from a different device or network

**If the problem persists**:
- Email: support@actplacemat.org.au
- Emergency line: +61-XXX-XXX-XXX
- Twitter: @ACTPlacemat (for status updates)

### Cultural Emergency

**Problem**: Inappropriate cultural content or serious protocol violation
**Immediate Actions**:
1. Report the content immediately using the report function
2. Contact Cultural Advisors: cultural-advisors@actplacemat.org.au
3. For serious violations: Elder Council emergency line +61-XXX-XXX-XXX

### Safety or Harassment Issues

**Problem**: Harassment, threats, or safety concerns
**Immediate Actions**:
1. Block the user immediately
2. Report using the platform's report function
3. Contact safety team: safety@actplacemat.org.au
4. For immediate danger: Contact local emergency services (000)

### Account Compromised

**Problem**: Someone else has access to your account
**Immediate Actions**:
1. Change your password immediately
2. Enable two-factor authentication
3. Review account activity and connections
4. Contact support: security@actplacemat.org.au

## üîê Account and Login Problems

### Can't Log In

**Forgot Password**
1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check email for reset link (including spam folder)
4. Follow instructions in reset email
5. Create a strong new password

**Account Locked**
- **Cause**: Multiple failed login attempts
- **Solution**: Wait 15 minutes and try again, or contact support
- **Prevention**: Use password manager, enable 2FA

**Email Not Recognized**
- **Check**: Verify you're using the correct email
- **Alternative**: Try logging in with username if you set one
- **Contact**: support@actplacemat.org.au if still unable to access

### Two-Factor Authentication Issues

**Lost Authenticator Device**
1. Use backup codes you saved during setup
2. Contact support with account verification details
3. Provide alternative authentication method
4. Support will help reset 2FA

**Authenticator App Not Working**
- **Check**: Time sync on your device
- **Try**: Manual time sync in authenticator app
- **Alternative**: Use SMS backup if configured
- **Contact**: support@actplacemat.org.au

### Profile and Settings Issues

**Can't Update Profile**
- **Clear browser cache** and try again
- **Check required fields** are completed
- **Image size**: Ensure profile photos are under 5MB
- **File format**: Use JPG, PNG, or GIF for images

**Settings Not Saving**
- **JavaScript enabled**: Ensure browser JavaScript is enabled
- **Network connection**: Check stable internet connection
- **Browser compatibility**: Use supported browser version
- **Contact support**: If problem persists

## üì± Technical Issues

### Website Performance

**Page Loading Slowly**
- **Internet connection**: Test speed at speedtest.net
- **Clear cache**: Clear browser cache and cookies
- **Browser update**: Ensure you're using latest browser version
- **Disable extensions**: Try disabling browser extensions
- **Different browser**: Try Chrome, Firefox, or Safari

**Images Not Loading**
- **Network issue**: Check internet connection
- **Ad blocker**: Disable ad blockers temporarily
- **Browser cache**: Clear browser cache
- **Image format**: Some older browsers don't support newer formats

**Features Not Working**
- **JavaScript**: Ensure JavaScript is enabled
- **Cookies**: Allow cookies for actplacemat.org.au
- **Pop-up blocker**: Disable for our site
- **Browser compatibility**: We support modern browsers (last 2 versions)

### Mobile App Issues

**App Won't Start**
- **Restart device**: Turn phone off and on again
- **Update app**: Check app store for updates
- **Free storage**: Ensure device has free storage space
- **Reinstall**: Delete and reinstall the app

**App Crashes Frequently**
- **Update app**: Install latest version
- **Restart device**: Reboot your phone
- **Free memory**: Close other running apps
- **Contact**: app-support@actplacemat.org.au with device details

**Notifications Not Working**
- **Check settings**: Ensure notifications are enabled in app
- **Phone settings**: Allow notifications for ACT Placemat
- **Do not disturb**: Check if device is in do not disturb mode
- **Battery optimization**: Exclude app from battery optimization

### Browser Compatibility

**Supported Browsers**

**Mobile Browsers**

## üåè Cultural Content Issues

### Content Review Status

**Story Under Review**
- **Normal process**: Cultural content review takes 1-5 business days
- **Elder review**: May take up to 2 weeks for sensitive content
- **Check status**: View submission status in your content dashboard
- **Contact**: cultural-advisors@actplacemat.org.au for status updates

**Content Rejected**
- **Review feedback**: Check the feedback provided with rejection
- **Understand reasons**: Cultural protocols, sensitivity, or guidelines
- **Revise content**: Make suggested changes and resubmit
- **Ask questions**: Contact Cultural Advisors for clarification

### Cultural Protocol Questions

**Unsure About Sharing**
- **When in doubt, don't**: Better to ask first
- **Consult advisors**: cultural-advisors@actplacemat.org.au
- **Community check**: Ask your community leaders
- **Research**: Review our Cultural Protocols Guide

**Attribution Questions**
- **Traditional Owner acknowledgment**: Always acknowledge when sharing cultural content
- **Permission**: Ensure you have permission to share
- **Credit**: Give proper credit to knowledge holders
- **Guidance**: Cultural Advisors can help with appropriate attribution

### Elder Review Process

**How It Works**
1. Content flagged for cultural sensitivity
2. Cultural Advisors conduct initial review
3. Elder Council consulted for sacred/sensitive content
4. Decision communicated to content creator
5. Appeals process available if needed

**Timeframes**
- Standard review: 2-3 business days
- Elder consultation: 5-14 business days
- Complex issues: May take longer with community consultation

## üîí Privacy and Safety

### Privacy Settings

**Who Can See My Content**
- **Public**: Anyone on the internet can see
- **Community**: Only community members can see
- **Connections**: Only your connections can see
- **Private**: Only you can see

**Controlling Contact**
- **Message settings**: Control who can send you messages
- **Connection requests**: Manage who can send connection requests
- **Notifications**: Control what notifications you receive
- **Blocking**: Block users to prevent all interaction

### Data and Privacy

**Downloading Your Data**
1. Go to Account Settings
2. Select "Privacy and Data"
3. Click "Download My Data"
4. Confirm your identity
5. Receive download link via email (may take 24-48 hours)

**Deleting Your Account**
1. Go to Account Settings
2. Select "Privacy and Data"
3. Click "Delete Account"
4. Confirm deletion (irreversible after 30 days)
5. Cultural data: May require community consultation

**Data Concerns**
- **Privacy questions**: privacy@actplacemat.org.au
- **Cultural data**: cultural-advisors@actplacemat.org.au
- **Data sovereignty**: Indigenous data rights respected

### Safety Features

**Reporting Inappropriate Content**
1. Click report button on content
2. Select violation type
3. Provide additional details
4. Submit report
5. Receive confirmation and follow-up

**Blocking Users**
- **How to block**: Click "..." menu on user profile, select "Block"
- **What it does**: Prevents all interaction and visibility
- **Unblocking**: Can be undone in privacy settings
- **Still see them**: Clear browser cache if you still see blocked content

## ‚ùì General Questions

### Platform Basics

**Q: Is ACT Placemat free to use?**
A: Yes, the platform is free for community members. We sustain ourselves through partnerships and ensure 40% of platform value returns to communities.

**Q: How do you make money if it's free?**
A: We partner with organizations for opportunity matching and community services. We don't sell user data or show advertising. 40% of our revenue goes directly back to communities.

**Q: What makes ACT Placemat different from other social platforms?**
A: We're specifically designed for community impact and respect Indigenous culture. We prioritize community benefit over engagement metrics and have strong cultural protocols.

### Cultural Questions

**Q: I'm not Indigenous - can I still use the platform?**
A: Yes! We welcome all people who respect our community guidelines and cultural protocols. The platform is for everyone committed to positive community impact.

**Q: How do I know if content I want to share is culturally appropriate?**
A: When in doubt, ask! Contact our Cultural Advisors or check our Cultural Protocols Guide. It's always better to ask first than apologize later.

**Q: What happens if I accidentally share something inappropriate?**
A: We understand mistakes happen. Contact our Cultural Advisors immediately, be open to learning, and follow guidance provided. We focus on education and relationship repair.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What devices can I use to access ACT Placemat?**
A: Any device with a modern web browser, plus our mobile apps for iOS and Android. We support accessibility features and assistive technologies.

**Q: Can I access my data if I leave the platform?**
A: Yes, you can download all your data at any time. For cultural data, we follow Indigenous data sovereignty principles which may require community consultation.

**Q: How do you protect my privacy?**
A: We use industry-standard security, provide granular privacy controls, and comply with Australian privacy laws plus Indigenous data sovereignty principles.

### Community Questions

**Q: How do I start a new community?**
A: Contact our Community Team who will help you set up your community space, establish guidelines, and invite members. Some communities require cultural advisor consultation.

**Q: Can I be in multiple communities?**
A: Yes! You can join as many communities as you like, though we encourage meaningful participation over quantity.

**Q: How do you handle conflicts between community members?**
A: We focus on restorative justice and relationship repair. Community moderators, cultural advisors, and mediation services help resolve conflicts constructively.

### Safety Questions

**Q: How do you keep the platform safe?**
A: We use a combination of community moderation, automated detection, cultural review processes, and user reporting. Safety is everyone's responsibility.

**Q: What should I do if I see inappropriate content?**
A: Report it using the report function. For cultural violations, also contact our Cultural Advisors. For safety issues, contact our Safety Team immediately.

**Q: How do you handle harassment?**
A: We take harassment seriously. We investigate all reports, provide support to affected users, and take appropriate action including removal and bans.
