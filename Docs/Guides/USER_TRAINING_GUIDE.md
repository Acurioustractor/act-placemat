## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit the Platform**: Navigate to https://app.actplacemat.org.au
2. **Sign Up**: Click "Create Account" and provide:
   - Valid email address
   - Secure password
   - Basic profile information
   - Community affiliation (optional)
3. **Verify Email**: Check your inbox and click the verification link
4. **Complete Profile**: Add additional details to help communities find you

### First Login

After account creation, you'll be guided through:
- Platform orientation tour
- Privacy preferences setup
- Community recommendations
- Story sharing introduction

## Sharing Your Story

### Story Creation Process

#### Planning Your Story
1. **Choose Your Purpose**: What do you want to achieve?
2. **Consider Your Audience**: Which communities will benefit?
3. **Gather Materials**: Photos, documents, or other media
4. **Structure Your Narrative**: Beginning, middle, and end

#### Creating Your Story

1. **Start a New Story**
   - Click "Share Story" from any page
   - Choose story type (personal, community, project)
   - Select relevant communities

2. **Add Content**
   - Write your narrative using the rich text editor
   - Upload images, videos, or documents
   - Add tags for discoverability
   - Set privacy preferences

3. **Review and Publish**
   - Preview your story
   - Check spelling and formatting

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Login Problems
**Issue**: Cannot access account
**Solutions**:
1. Check email and password
2. Use "Forgot Password" feature
3. Clear browser cache and cookies
4. Try a different browser
5. Contact support if issues persist

#### Content Upload Issues
**Issue**: Cannot upload photos/videos
**Solutions**:
1. Check file size (max 10MB for images, 50MB for videos)
2. Verify supported formats (JPG, PNG, MP4, MOV)
3. Ensure stable internet connection
4. Try uploading one file at a time
5. Contact support for persistent issues

#### Community Access
**Issue**: Cannot join or access community
**Solutions**:
1. Check if membership approval is required
2. Review community eligibility requirements
3. Contact community moderators
4. Ensure your profile is complete
5. Respect waiting periods for approval

#### Performance Issues
**Issue**: Platform running slowly
**Solutions**:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Close unnecessary browser tabs
3. Clear browser cache
4. Update your browser
5. Try accessing during off-peak hours

Control who can see:
