## üéØ **KEY DIFFERENCES EXPLAINED**

### **Dashboard vs Intelligence Tools:**
- **Dashboards** = Show you what's happening (data visualization)
- **Intelligence Tools** = Tell you what it means and what to do (AI analysis)

### **Empathy Ledger vs Stories Intelligence:**
- **Empathy Ledger** = Raw story archive (browse, search, manage stories)
- **Stories Intelligence** = AI analysis of stories (themes, patterns, insights)

### **Community Insights vs Real Dashboard:**
- **Real Dashboard** = Current community metrics and activity
- **Community Insights** = Deep analysis of community patterns and needs

### **Multiple Intelligence Tools:**
- Each focuses on a different data source (Gmail, LinkedIn, Stories, etc.)
- **Unified Intelligence** combines insights from all sources
