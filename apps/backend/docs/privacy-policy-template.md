# Privacy Policy - ACT Platform

**Effective Date:** [DATE]  
**Last Updated:** [DATE]

## Introduction

ACT Platform ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform and services.

We comply with:
- Australian Privacy Principles (Privacy Act 1988)
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- Indigenous Data Sovereignty principles

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We collect information you provide directly to us:
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, profile details
- **User Content:** Stories, projects, comments, and other content you create
- **Communication Data:** Messages sent through our platform
- **Professional Information:** Organisation affiliations, skills, interests

### Automatically Collected Information
We automatically collect certain information:
- **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, operating system
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform
- **Location Data:** General geographic location (with your consent)
- **Cookies and Tracking:** See our Cookie Policy for details

### Sensitive Information
We may collect sensitive information with your explicit consent:
- **Cultural Information:** Indigenous heritage, cultural affiliations
- **Health Information:** Only if relevant to community projects
- **Financial Information:** For payment processing and grants

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Purposes
- **Platform Services:** Provide and improve our platform functionality
- **Account Management:** Create and maintain your user account
- **Communications:** Send you updates, notifications, and newsletters
- **Community Building:** Connect you with relevant projects and people
- **Support Services:** Respond to inquiries and provide customer support

### Secondary Purposes
- **Analytics:** Understand how our platform is used and improve services
- **Research:** Conduct research for social impact and platform development
- **Security:** Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
- **Legal Compliance:** Meet legal obligations and enforce our terms

### Cultural Safety
All data processing respects Indigenous data sovereignty principles:
- Community consent for cultural content sharing
- Elder review processes for sensitive cultural information
- Cultural safety scoring to protect sacred knowledge
- Community-controlled data governance where applicable

## Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information based on:
- **Consent:** You have given clear consent for specific processing activities
- **Contract:** Processing is necessary for our service delivery
- **Legal Obligation:** We must process data to comply with legal requirements
- **Vital Interests:** Processing is necessary to protect life or health
- **Public Interest:** Activities serve legitimate public interest
- **Legitimate Interest:** Processing serves our legitimate business interests while respecting your rights

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### Third-Party Service Providers
We share information with trusted service providers:
- **Database Hosting:** Supabase (encrypted data storage)
- **Payment Processing:** Stripe (payment information only)
- **Analytics:** Anonymous usage analytics only
- **Security Services:** Cloudflare (traffic and security data)

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose information when required by law:
- Court orders or legal process
- Law enforcement requests with proper authority
- Protection of rights, property, or safety
- Emergency situations requiring immediate action

### Business Transfers
In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity with equivalent privacy protections.

### No Selling of Personal Data
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access Rights
You have the right to:
- **Know:** What personal information we have about you
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal information
- **Update:** Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
- **Delete:** Request deletion of your personal information ("Right to be Forgotten")
- **Restrict:** Limit how we use your personal information
- **Object:** Opt out of certain processing activities
- **Portability:** Export your data in machine-readable format

### How to Exercise Your Rights
Contact us at privacy@act.place or use the privacy controls in your account settings:
- **Data Export:** Download your complete data archive
- **Account Deletion:** Delete your account and all associated data
- **Consent Management:** Update your consent preferences
- **Communication Preferences:** Manage email and notification settings

### Response Timeframes
We respond to privacy requests within:
- **Simple requests:** 7 business days
- **Complex requests:** 30 days (may extend to 60 days with notification)
- **Urgent security issues:** 24-48 hours

## Data Security

### Technical Safeguards
We implement comprehensive security measures:
- **Encryption:** AES-256-GCM encryption for sensitive data
- **Transmission Security:** HTTPS/TLS 1.2+ for all data transmission
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access limitations
- **Security Headers:** HSTS, CSP, and other protective headers
- **Regular Audits:** Internal and external security assessments

### Organisational Safeguards
- **Staff Training:** Regular privacy and security training
- **Background Checks:** Security clearances for staff with data access
- **Incident Response:** Documented breach response procedures
- **Vendor Management:** Privacy requirements for all suppliers

### Data Breach Response
In case of a data breach, we will:
- **Immediate Action:** Contain the breach within 1 hour
- **Assessment:** Evaluate scope and risk within 24 hours
- **Notification:** Notify authorities within 72 hours if required
- **User Communication:** Inform affected users if high risk exists
- **Remediation:** Fix vulnerabilities and strengthen security

## International Data Transfers

### Data Location
Your data is primarily stored in:
- **Australia:** Primary data centres in Sydney and Melbourne
- **Backup Locations:** Encrypted backups in Singapore and Ireland
- **Processing:** Some processing may occur in trusted international locations

### Transfer Safeguards
International transfers are protected by:
- **Adequacy Decisions:** Transfers only to countries with adequate protection
- **Standard Contractual Clauses:** EU-approved contract terms
- **Binding Corporate Rules:** Internal data protection policies
- **Consent:** Your explicit consent for specific transfers

## Data Retention

### Retention Periods
We retain your information for:
- **Active Accounts:** Duration of account plus 2 years
- **User Content:** 5 years after account closure
- **Financial Records:** 7 years (legal requirement)
- **Security Logs:** 5 years for compliance
- **Marketing Data:** Until consent is withdrawn

### Deletion Process
When retention periods expire, we:
- **Secure Deletion:** Overwrite data multiple times
- **Third-Party Notification:** Ensure partners delete shared data
- **Audit Trail:** Maintain deletion records for compliance
- **Verification:** Confirm complete data removal

## Children's Privacy

### Age Requirements
- **Minimum Age:** 13 years old
- **Parental Consent:** Required for users under 16
- **Enhanced Protection:** Additional safeguards for users under 18

### Special Protections
For users under 18, we:
- **Limit Data Collection:** Collect only necessary information
- **Restrict Sharing:** No third-party sharing without parental consent
- **Enhanced Security:** Additional security measures
- **Regular Review:** Review and delete data when no longer needed
