# ACT Placemat - Product Overview

## What is ACT Placemat?

ACT Placemat is an interactive web application for visualizing and managing community projects with real-time Notion database integration. It serves as a "project overview" dashboard for A Curious Tractor (ACT), a community-focused organization working across five main areas.

## Core Purpose

- **Community Transparency**: Provide clear visibility into all ACT projects and initiatives
- **Project Management**: Track project status, funding, and progress across different areas
- **Community Engagement**: Enable community members to understand and engage with ongoing work
- **Data Sovereignty**: Ensure communities maintain control over their stories and data

## Five Main Project Areas

1. **Story & Sovereignty**: Community-controlled narratives and data ownership
2. **Economic Freedom**: Community ownership and cooperative economics  
3. **Community Engagement**: Participatory democracy and mutual aid
4. **Operations & Infrastructure**: Systems and tools for community resilience
5. **Research & Development**: Community-led innovation and experimentation

## Key Features

- Interactive area cards for top-level navigation
- Progressive disclosure through hierarchical dropdowns
- Real-time Notion database synchronization
- Advanced filtering by status, funding type, and other criteria
- Responsive design for desktop and mobile
- Community-focused project visualization

## Community Values

- **Community Ownership**: 40% of profits flow back to communities
- **Decentralized Power**: Community-led decision making
- **Truth-Telling**: Ethical storytelling with consent management
- **Economic Justice**: Supporting cooperative and community-owned models
- **Cultural Protocols**: Respecting Indigenous and traditional knowledge systems
